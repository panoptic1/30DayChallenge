Hello everyone! This is simply a place for us to connect on a first-name basis while we all endeavor to stay sober througout the month of October.

If you'd like, give yourself a brief introduction in your first entry.

Fun Fact: The 'Delete Entry' button that appears on every entry will actually delete your entry from the database connected to the site. Poof!

As such, please refrain from hitting the delete button on other people's entries!
